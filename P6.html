Published fine dust digital news for youth

Web development internship for Joongang daily in korea

ROLE

1) Web development

SKILLS UTILIZED

Coding(javaScript, jQuery)

Since many youths do not read newspapers or watch news on television, in this internship we made several pieces of digital news converted from newspaper articles for the youths. Because the fine dust situation in Korea has deteriorated in recent years, all of the works we created during the internship program were about fine dust. All the works below were published on the official website of the newspaper company.

About

I’m a PhD student working on Human-Robot Interaction (HRI) and AI-driven social robots in a clinical setting. I design and develop playful robot systems to support children’s communication and emotional development.

Projects

  • LLM-Driven Robot for adaptable System – Therapist-centered system design for sustainable robot system.
  • Robot-based Speech Therapy Games – Co-designed games with therapists to support children with language impairments.
  • Emotion Modulation AI – Explored contextual affect in humans and deep neural networks.

Publications

  • Contextual modulation of affect: Comparing humans and deep neural networks. Affective Computing and Cognitive Science, 2024.

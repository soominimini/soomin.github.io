An Android application matches crowdfunding products to users based on their personality

Work from the Fitech Hackathon

ROLE

1) Design the whole process of application

2) Android application development

3) IBM watson API communicate

SKILLS UTILIZED

Coding(JAVA, python)

SOFTWARE

IBM Watson Personality insight API

Purpose of this project

The purpose of this application can be divided into two parts. The first part is collecting the bank customers' psychometric credit information. And the second is getting people to participate in the personality test. To complete those goals, we made a crowdfunding platform that enables customers to take a psychometric credit test willingly. To maximize the use of personality information, we matched a user's personality test result to categories of crowdfunding products and services.

Why Big5 personality test?

We chose a test from the Visual DNA company for assessing personality based on the various studies and the efficacy of actual applications in the financial industry. Visual DNA has developed a personality test based on the Big5 personality test model that enables inferring a user's financial credit from their personality test. EFL, in addition to Visual DNA, has developed its own personality assessment test to assess the user's financial credibility. Their work was utilized in the financial industry. For example, Mastercard lowered its loan repayment failure rate by 23% by applying the personality test of visual DNA. Also, the BTPN Bank of Indonesia reduced the loan repayment failure rate by 31% after applying the EFL personality assessment test. In this hackathon, we suggested a crowdfunding platform to recommend products based on the users' personalities while enabling the bank to collect users' personality data to assess their financial credit.

the system flow 

1) Sign up for an account 
2) Navigate to the Explore tab
3) Click the gray banner for the Big-5 personality test
4) Take a test consisting of 15 questions
5) Check out the recommended crowdfunding products and services in the Explore tab
6) Funding through a bank account 
7) Go to the personal information tab and see all the products the user has funded so far
